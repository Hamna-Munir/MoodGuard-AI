MoodGuard
MoodGuard uses deep learning to detect 7 emotional states through facial expressions in real time. It provides personalized mental health insights and session analytics to help students and professionals monitor their emotional wellbeing proactively.

The Problem
1 in 4 people worldwide face mental health challenges. MoodGuard provides early detection through passive emotion monitoring using just your camera.
⚙️
How It Works
A CNN trained on 35,000+ facial images detects 7 emotions. Personalized mental health tips generated based on your detected emotional state.
🔒
Privacy First
100% local processing. No images or data sent anywhere. Your emotional data never leaves your machine.
